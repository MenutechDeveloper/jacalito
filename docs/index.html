Jacalito Family Restaurant


Jacalito is a Mexican restaurant located in Strong City, Kansas, proudly run by the Soria family, who moved from Texas to Kansas in 2003 and began opening various restaurants throughout the region.

In 2014, they decided to open a new restaurant that would offer a variety of traditional dishes from different regions of Mexico, as well as American cuisine and vegetarian options—aimed at meeting the needs of the Chase County community. We take pride in serving freshly prepared food every day, with quality and great flavor.

We also offer catering services for all types of events, along with desserts and our delicious margaritas—Mexican drinks available in a variety of flavors.

Proud to be both Mexican and part of the Chase County community, the owners of Jacalito warmly welcome both locals and travelers. It will be our pleasure to serve you.

"We are an unforgettable culinary experience."
